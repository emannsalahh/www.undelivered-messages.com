The book "Undelivered messages" is a result of a 30-day journey where people had the chance to talk about a relationship that affected their life and express their feelings about it. In addition, they were given the chance to send a message to the other party of this relationship. These messages were visualized in a way that expresses the message and the feelings associated with it. The aim of "Undelivered Messages" book is to explore the effect of relationships on human life and how complex human feelings are beyond the word "Love" or "Hate."
